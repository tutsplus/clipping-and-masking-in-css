background-clip: border-box;

Border-box will allow the background to extend beneath the border.

background-clip: padding-box;

Padding-box will allow the background to extend to the inside edges of the border.

background-clip: content-box;

Content-box will clip the background to the edges of the content so it disregards any margin or padding you might have.

background-clip: text;

The background is clipped to or is painted within the text.
